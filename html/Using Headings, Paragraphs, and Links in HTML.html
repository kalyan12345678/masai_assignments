Jupyter notebook, Scikit-learn, Python, NumPy, Pandas. Cancer Disease Detection is about diagnosing Breast Cancer (BC) disease through dataset of benign & malignant disease. The early diagnosis of BC can improve the prognosis and chance of survival significantly, as it can promote timely clinical treatment to patients. Further accurate classification of benign tumors can prevent patients undergoing unnecessary treatments. Thus, the correct diagnosis of BC and classification of patients into malignant or benign groups is the subject of much research

Automated Hydroponic Agriculture System

C, Arduino IDE, Flutter Framework, Firebase Realtime Database. An automated hydroponic agriculture system uses technology to grow plants without soil. It circulates a nutrient-rich water solution to the plant roots, and includes components like a water pump, lighting, climate control, and automation sensors. Benefits include higher yield, water conservation, faster growth, space efficiency, and reduced pesticide use. Developing the system requires expertise in engineering, agriculture, automation, programming, and project management.
